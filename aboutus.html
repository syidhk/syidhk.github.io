About our philosophy

Service Philosophy

Company founder Dr Hui-wen Yang always think: "the designer shall be submitted to the most suitable for customers professional design, and provide the best service all the way, to meet customer demand at the same time, our thoughts and our wealth." Under the influence of this thought, we always emphasize the professional ability and professionalism of the design team, and elevate the customer service to the same important status as the design quality and level.

Therefore, we do not pursue the company size and number of expanded constantly, but appropriate control of the company's development speed and team size, but also take the initiative to choose the customer, and limit the number of business at the same time, to ensure that each project must have enough professional team to special is responsible for, and are equipped with a considerable design ability can be competent for complete; It also ensures that Dr. Yang has enough time to personally participate in the relevant design, guidance and approval.

Therefore, once we have identified the partners and projects, we will focus on providing timely and professional services to our customers.

We are not the biggest and probably not the best, but we must be a professional design company that can provide the most suitable design products and services. This is the goal of every designer, every service, every project.
